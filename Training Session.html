⚽ FC Goa Training Session⚽

Special Session for Best Students

Session Plan

  • Warm-up (10 min): Light jogging, stretching, fun passing games.
  • Skill Practice (20 min): Dribbling around cones, short passes, ball control.
  • Team Drill (20 min): 5 vs 5 mini match with simple rules.
  • Fun Activity (15 min): Penalty shootout competition.
  • Cool Down (10 min): Stretching and group reflection.

Important Notes

  • Bring your football shoes and water bottle.
  • Wear your school sports uniform.
  • Respect teammates and play fair.
  • Focus on learning, not just winning.
